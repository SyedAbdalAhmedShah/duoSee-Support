Frequently Asked Questions

Which iPhones are supported?

DuoSee requires iPhone XS or newer, as dual-camera recording depends on hardware available from that model onward.

Where are my videos saved?

Your recordings are saved locally to your device and can be exported to your Photos library from the export screen.

What does DuoSee Pro include?

Pro unlocks all layouts, 4K recording, unlimited recording duration, and watermark-free exports.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the Pro screen and tap "Restore Purchases."

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings → your Apple ID → Subscriptions → DuoSee, and tap Cancel.
