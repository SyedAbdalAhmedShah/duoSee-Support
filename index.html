Support

Frequently asked questions

Which iPhones are supported?

DuoSee requires iPhone XS or newer, as dual-camera recording depends on hardware available from that model onward.

Where are my videos saved?

Your recordings are saved locally to your device and can be exported to your Photos library from the export screen.

What does DuoSee Pro include?

Pro unlocks all layouts, 4K recording, unlimited recording duration, and watermark-free exports.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the Pro screen and tap “Restore Purchases.”

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings → your Apple ID → Subscriptions → DuoSee, and tap Cancel.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026

DuoSee (“the app”, “we”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what the app accesses and how your information is handled. In short: your videos and photos stay on your device.

Camera and Microphone

DuoSee uses your device’s cameras and microphone to record dual-camera videos and photos. This capture happens entirely on your device. We do not access, collect, transmit, or store your camera or microphone input on any server.

Photos Library

When you export a video or photo, DuoSee saves it to your device’s Photos library with your permission. We do not read, upload, or access other content in your library.

Your Content

All recordings and exports created with DuoSee are stored locally on your device. Your content is never uploaded to us and is never shared with third parties by the app.

Subscriptions and Purchases

DuoSee offers an optional Pro subscription. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. To manage subscriptions and unlock Pro features across your devices, we use RevenueCat, a subscription management provider. For this purpose, a random, anonymous identifier and your purchase/transaction status may be processed. This information is used only to deliver the features you paid for. We do not receive your name, email, or Apple ID, and we do not use this data for advertising.

  • Apple’s handling of purchases is covered by Apple’s Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat’s handling of subscription data is covered by RevenueCat’s Privacy Policy.

Data We Do Not Collect

DuoSee does not collect your personal information, does not track you across other apps or websites, and does not sell your data. There are no third-party advertising trackers in the app.

Children’s Privacy

DuoSee is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date above.
